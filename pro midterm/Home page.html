WELCOME TO APPLIED SCIENCE PRIVATE UNIVERSITY

ASU in Brief

The Applied Science Private University (ASU), also known as the University of All Arabs, was established in 1989, becoming one of the pioneering private sector universities in Jordan. It later became an integral part of the Arab International Company for Education and Investment.

The academic journey commenced in October 1991 when the university welcomed its first cohort of students across three colleges: Arts and Humanities, Science, and Economics. These colleges collectively offered 14 majors, fostering pride in the university's graduates worldwide.
